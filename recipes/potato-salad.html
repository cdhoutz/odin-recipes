Old Fashioned Potato Salad

Description

This is a delicious and versatile potato salad recipe. Great for portlucks, picnics, or an easy side to many meals. This recipe yeilds 8 servings.

Ingredients

  • 5 potatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup sweet pickle relish
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic salt
  • ¼ teaspoon celery salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
